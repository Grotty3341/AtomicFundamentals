Intro Into Atomic Fundamentals
There are many things realating to atomic fundamentals, including atomic models, creating energy and even making weapons, but they all require one thing, the atom. The atom is the smallest type of matter that retains all of the chemical properties of an element. Atoms make up everything in our universe, from you to our clothes to our planet. There are 3 subatomic particles, these particles: electrons, protons, neutrons make up atoms. Electrons, are the particles circling the nucleus and Neutrons and Protons are surrounding the nucleus.
